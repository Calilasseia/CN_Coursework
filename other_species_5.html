The Invertebrate Fauna Of Norton Priory



Mayfly

Specimen observed & photographed at grid reference SJ 5490 8351 (VC 58), on 2020/07/23


Despite having subjected the site to regular sampling for invertebrates since 2014, I had to wait until July 2020 to see my first Mayfly at a moth trap. This particular individual is awaiting identification to species level, as Mayflies are not a speciality of mine!
