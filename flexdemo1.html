Notice in the above examples, that a choice of primary ordering axis is made ("row" for horizontal, "column" for vertical), and that contained elements are ordered along that axis first. If the contained elements extend to more than one row or column, then they are ordered again along that secondary ordering axis, after the primary ordering has bene performed.
