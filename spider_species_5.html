The Invertebrate Fauna Of Norton Priory



Theridion varians.

Specimen observed & photographed at grid reference SJ 5488 8354 (VC 58), on 2019/11/24


This particularly attractive (and tiny!) spider is a lowland and coastal specialist, which accounts for it being found at a site within a short distance of the River Mersey. This particular specimen was right at the limit of the ability of my camera to zoom in on small specimens, with a leg span of just 5 millimetres.
