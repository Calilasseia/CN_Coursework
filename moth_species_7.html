The Invertebrate Fauna Of Norton Priory



Bordered Beauty (Epione repandaria)

Specimen observed & photographed at grid reference SJ 5490 8351 (VC 58), on 2020/07/23


Just a decade ago, this species would have been considered a spectacular find in the Runcorn area, as its range at that time was restricted to locations in the south of England. However, climate change has led to a dramatic northward expansion of its range, and the species can now be found in many areas in the north-west of England. NOTE: This species has a relative, the Dark Bordered Beauty (Epione vespertaria) that is a Red Data Book species.
