The Invertebrate Fauna Of Norton Priory



Deraeocoris lutescens

Specimen observed & photographed at grid reference SJ 5490 8351 (VC 58), on 2020/09/04


Deraeocoris lutescens is one of several species of predatory bug in the Genus Deraeocoris, that are found in various habitats, and some of which may occasionally appear in suburban gardens, hunting for prey such as aphids, small flies and beetles, and some other bug species such as members of the Family Miridae (plant bugs) The specimen depicted here is a nymph, the adult has fully developed wings in contrast to the nymph in the photograph.
