The Invertebrate Fauna Of Norton Priory



Green Silver-Lines (Pseudoips prasinana)

Specimen observed & photographed at grid reference SJ 5480 8353 (VC 58), on 2021/06/13


I first observed this species at, of all places, the giant ICI Rocksavage chemical plant in Runcorn, but individuals have intermittently appeared at Norton Priory since 2016. Fresh specimens are an intense jade green colour, with somewhat fuzzy silvery-white lines overlaid upon the basic green colour of the wings. Favourite larval foodplants of this species are Oak and Birch, but the larvae will feed upon the leaves of several other deciduous trees.
